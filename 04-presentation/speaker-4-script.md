# Speaker 4 Script: Policy Recommendations

## Time Allocation
**Total: 3 minutes 30 seconds**

## Script

### [SLIDE 16 - Our Three Policies] [0:00-0:30]

"You've now heard the three bottlenecks: a grid that can't connect clean energy, a nitrogen crisis that blocks construction, and a workforce gap that slows everything down.

We didn't start with policies and look for problems. We started with bottlenecks and designed a key for each lock.

Three policies. €17 billion. Each one targets a specific constraint:

[PAUSE]

Let me explain each one."

**[SLIDE 17 - Policy 1: Grid Modernization] [0:30-1:30]**

"Our first policy is the **Grid Modernization Fund**—€10 billion over five years to expand transmission capacity, deploy smart grid technology, and build energy storage.

The problem is clear: over 14,000 projects representing 9 gigawatts of capacity are stuck on waiting lists—some for 7-10 years.

Our solution: €10 billion public investment that will unlock an estimated €25 billion in total investment (every €1 public brings €2-3 private).

This addresses our number one bottleneck and enables all renewable deployment to proceed.

The timeline: major projects complete by 2030, full deployment by 2031."

**[SLIDE 18 - Policy 2: Agricultural Transition] [1:30-2:15]**

"Our second policy is the **Agricultural Transition Incentive Program**—€5 billion over seven years with three voluntary pathways:

- **Pathway A:** Voluntary buyout at 120% market value for farms near Natura 2000—land becomes nature reserves
- **Pathway B:** 50% grants for precision agriculture technology—reduce emissions while farming
- **Pathway C:** Subsidies for farm diversification, including solar on barns

This is voluntary, not mandatory. We're working with farmers, not against them.

Target: 10,500 farms transitioned, 50,000 hectares restored to nature."

**[SLIDE 19 - Policy 3: Green Skills] [2:15-3:00]**

"Our third policy is the **Green Skills Initiative**—€2 billion to reskill 50,000 workers for green economy jobs.

We provide:
- Free tuition for 6-18 month certifications
- 80% of previous wage during training (up to 18 months)
- €5,000 employer tax credit for hiring graduates

Focus regions: Groningen (gas transition), farming provinces

This addresses the jobs question: the transition creates as many jobs as it changes—if we have the skills to fill them."

**[SLIDE 20 - Synergies] [3:00-3:30]**

"**A farmer in Gelderland, a grid worker in Groningen, a solar installer in Rotterdam—our three policies connect their futures.**

Grid expansion enables farm solar. Grid construction needs the workforce we train. Agricultural workers transition to green jobs. These policies are not three separate programs—they're three parts of one system.

[TRANSITION TO SPEAKER 5]

We've shown you what we propose and how the pieces fit together. But there's one more question: could the Netherlands do this alone? My colleague will explain why the answer is no—and why that's actually good news for our policy."
